about

Oppih is a software plugin that records your AI interactions and saves them directly to your computer. It currently integrates with ChatGPT: future support planned for all major LLMs.

Users can capture full ChatGPT threads with a single click. Each session is saved as a plain .txt file to your device, allowing you to retain ownership of intellectual property that would otherwise stay locked inside proprietary platforms.

It gives users a second-opinion into how outputs are shaped and inputs are interpreted, unlocking new ways to learn, debug, and better understand how large language models work.

Oppih is a free tool and we do not accept compensation for access to this application.
