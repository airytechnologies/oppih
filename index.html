What is Oppih?

Local Record of AI Interactions

Oppih is a place where users can have their own record of interactions they have with AI. The first version of Oppih works alongside ChatGPT, with the prospect of any LLMs being supported in future versions.

Transcribe

In addition to recording in real-time through a blockchain-based system, we offer the ability to transcribe entire threads and save them directly to your local drive — nothing stored in the cloud unless you choose to.

Chain of Thought

When recording in real-time, users can enable 'logic' — exposing reasoning, expected weights, and cognitive patterns to better understand what’s being received and said. **requires openAI API key

Reflective Intelligence

With all of this information, users can reiterate this back to their AI for reflective purpose. This could be used for countless reasons: realign drifted responses, improve prompt clarity, remind the AI of certain details that may have been forgotten, and more!
